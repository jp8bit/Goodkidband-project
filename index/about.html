Introduction

Good Kid is a Toronto-based indie rock band known for their energetic sound and viral streaming presence. The five-piece—vocalist Nick Frosst, drummer Jon Kereliuk, bassist Michael Kozakov, and guitarists David Wood and Jacob Tsafatinos—rose to fame after their music became a staple in Fortnite streams, thanks to their no-DMCA policy. Their mascot, Nomu Kid, stars in their visuals. With four EPs to their name (Good Kid 1-4), hits like Mimi's Delivery Service and a 100M+-streamed cover of Laufey's From the Start showcase their genre-blending appeal.
